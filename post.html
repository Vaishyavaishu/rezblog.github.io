Is Augmented Reality the future?

Augmented reality (AR) is one of the biggest technology trends right now, and it’s only going to get bigger as AR ready smartphones and other devices become more accessible around the world .

Augmented Reality has increased human opportunities with automation and machine learning with advanced knowledge. AR works as a digital revolution that is offering new opportunities and information that were unknown.

The fact is that humans are misinterpreting the evolution of technology in the present world. The algorithms and machines are not smart enough to replace human strengths that include sophisticated motor skills.The fact is that AR let us see the environment right infront of us and provide us better understanding of the environment with the help of technology.Now we could see that the augmented reality is used readily now in different platforms Snapchat lenses, in apps that help you find your car in a crowded parking lot, and in variety of shopping apps that let you try on clothes without even leaving home and shop online and in many games to fascinate certain users and improve their experiences.

Actually Augmented Reality uses real world environment and projects the virtual experience on top of it for improved insight of the users. Now it is believed that Apple and Facebook MNCs are going to bring sunglasses as their new product which has Augmented Reality technology embedded in it that makes user to explore things they could actually not in the real world. From recent emerging trends in computer technology we could see that AR/VR technology has existed for a couple of years and its adoption at scale needs 5G and edge computing. The ultra-low latency and high bandwidth that 5G brings is crucial in enabling the AR/VR use cases.
